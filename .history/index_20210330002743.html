Cover letter

My name is Dylan Reimer, and I am an Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Undergraduate at the University of California, Berkeley. I am currently pursuing undergraduate research opportunities, collaborative projects, and Summer 2021 internships.

My passion for learning and engineering has led me to pursue many projects over the last few years, and I’ve grown as a result of each experience. My endeavors in designing and building modular synthesizers show my initiative to explore unfamiliar physical and digital environments and conceptualize a project from idea to the final product. DrummerBoy taught me that collaboration is the key to successful engineering endeavors - my collaborators and I used our varying perspectives and skillsets to tackle looming problems from different angles. My goal is always to use every project and experience, whether a success or fail, to learn and grow.
